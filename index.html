Nimit Mehra

I'm Nimit, Co-Founder of Zeo, where we're helping SMB businesses streamline their fleet operations. Our work impacts thousands of companies involved in deliveries and field services. 

With a background spanning venture product management and finance, I bring a unique perspective to building and scaling technology businesses. I'm a CFA Charterholder and Y Combinator alum (W20), passionate about creating products that solve real-world problems at scale.

Previously, I was part of the early team at Jio, where I witnessed and contributed to the hypergrowth from a few thousand to 300 million users—an experience that shaped my approach to building scalable systems and user-centric products. I've also worked in private banking at Standard Chartered, giving me deep insights into financial markets and corporate strategy.

When I'm not building products or mentoring founders, you'll find me exploring new cities, reading about behavioural economics, philosophy, international politics, technology or experimenting with new productivity systems.

Feel free to reach out if you're working on something interesting in logistics, technology, AI, building in emerging markets, or just want to chat about startups.
